ZOOM – R8

GLOBAL
Jog Wheel Use Jog Wheel to navigate in Arrangement View
F1 + Jog Wheel Hold F1 to activate Zoom then use Jog Wheel to Zoom in and out
F1 + F2 Hold F1 then press F2 to Zom in Fit
F1 + F5 + B1-8 Hold F1 then hold F5 then press Button 1-8 to start Scene
F5 + F4 Hold F5 then press F4 to increase Scene Bank
F5 + F3 Hold F5 then press F3 to decrease Scene Bank
REW Rewind
FF Fast Forward
STOP Stop / Go to Begin
GLOBAL
PLAY Toggle Play
REC Toggle Record
Cursor Navigate in Bitwig (ENTER doesnt work)
D2 Toggle between Mixer Mode and MIDI Mode
MIDI MODE
B1-8 Free assignable to any Parameter (B9 "MASTER" is not assignable)
F1-9 Free assignable to any Parameter
F2 Press F2 to increase Bank
F2 + F1 Hold F2 then press F1 to decrease Bank
MIXER MODE
D1 Toggle between REC, SOLO and MUTE Mode
B1-8 Toggle Rec, SOLO or MUTE
F1-F8 Change Volume
F9 Change Volume of Master Track
D3 + F1-F8 Hold D3 then use Faders to change Panorama
D3 + F9 Hold D3 then use Fader 9 "MASTER" to change Master Track Panorama
F2 Press F2 to increase Track Bank
F2 + F1 Hold F2 then press F1 to decrease Track Bank
